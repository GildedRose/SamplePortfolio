About Me

Anne Plummer is a photographer & content creator specializing in lifestyle and photojournalistic styles.Her experiences include live events for national brands, social media campaigns for nonprofits, and families who desire artfully captured candid moments. Her work has been seen on Good Morning America, Cheddar, and other national and internal publications.

She is currently enrolled in the UT Austin Full Stack Development Bootcamp. Her goal is to expand her freelance offerings with development projects for clients.
